KlearFresh

The Kleartrace Program

Special program optimized for the Food Industry to address manufacturers’ compliance needs with FSMA and prevent spoilage of fresh product in transit.

KlarityFresh

What is KlarityFresh?

• Highly affordable disposable trackers
>> Simple and sleek trackers fit into any package
>> ~0.2% of the cost of shipment
• Real-time monitoring of temperature and location in transit
• Specifciations

Who should use it?

• Manufacturers of fresh products that want to prevent temperature abuse during transport
• Most relevant for manufacturers using LTL or third-party carriers

Why?

• Take immediate action when product is out of temp to prevent spoilage
• Protect your brand
• Comply with new FSMA regulations of temperature integrity during transport
• Use data to manage performance of carriers and lanes

KlarityConnect

What is KlarityConnect?

• High functioning, reusable, trackers with multiple sensors:
>> Temperature
>> Humidity
>> Shock
>> Tilt
• Rechargeable batteries with device life upto a year
• Rechargeable batteries with device life upto a year
• Real-time monitoring of temperature and location in transit

Who should use it?

• Manufacturers with dedicated truck loads and deep relationships with their distributor partner
• Carriers and distributors interested in improving the quality of product handling in transport

Why?

• Take immediate action when product is out of temp to prevent spoilage
• Protect your brand
• Proof of handling to suppliers and customers
• Driver management and training
• Data and analytics to improve operations
