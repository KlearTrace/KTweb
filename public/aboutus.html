Our Purpose

KlearTrace exists to bring clarity to the global food and drug supply chain.

Our innovative solutions combine deep industry knowledge with the latest in Mobility, Cloud infrastructure, IOT sensors and NB-IOT and Cat-M Communication Technology to help all participants of the perishable supply chain improve:

• Supply chain visibility
• Traceability
• Temperature control
• Regulatory compliance

Our solutions have a direct impact on our customers’ bottomline by reducing spoilage.

We believe reducing spoilage is good for business and good for the planet. We believe that food quality and safety has a direct impact on the health and wellness of our communities. We are on a mission to use technology and analytics to help food iand drug industry participants deliver on their mission of providing great food experiences and personalized medicine.
